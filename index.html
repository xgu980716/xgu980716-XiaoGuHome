About me

When I was in high school in China, I first realized the power of new media. At that time, I wanted to use new media create my stuff. Coming to study in the United States gave me an opportunity to make art with new media. I believed new media will be the primary form of art in the future. During my time abroad, I also felt my strong sense of nationality. Therefore, I insist on combining Chinese art and Western art into my work now. I want my work to serve as a window for western audiences to learn about China.

For my works now, I focus on how to combine different art genre into one piece. I would like to create my own style. I also hope my work can be a bridge between different cultures.
